Welcome to TNGB PRIDE

Empowering SC/ST/SCA Staff Members of TNGB with Pride and Purpose At TNGB PRIDE, we believe in fostering a community where Progressiveness, Resilience, Inclusivity, Dignity, and Empowerment are not just ideals but our guiding principles. Our mission is to create a robust platform for SC/ST/SCA staff members to unite, thrive, and achieve their true potential. By promoting transparency, fairness, and ethical leadership, we aim to build a future where every member feels valued, heard, and empowered.

About Us

Who We Are TNGB PRIDE stands for Promoting Progressive, Resilience, Inclusivity, Dignity, and Empowerment of SC/ST/SCA staff members of TNGB. Born out of the necessity for transparency, accountability, and ethical leadership, TNGB PRIDE represents a united and proactive effort to uplift and empower our community.

Our Vision


A thriving community where every member experiences dignity, equity, and empowerment in the workplace, driving progress for generations to come.

Our Mission


To build a platform that ensures the voices of SC/ST/SCA staff members are heard, respected, and elevated. By fostering inclusivity, transparency, and meaningful change, we aim to secure a brighter and more stable future for all our members.

Our Values

Progressiveness

Promoting forward-thinking initiatives that create opportunities and drive growth.

Dignity

Upholding respect, fairness, and integrity in all our actions.

Empowerment

Providing tools, resources, and advocacy for members to excel.

Inclusivity

Ensuring every voice matters and fostering a sense of belonging.
